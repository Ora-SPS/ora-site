For help with the Ora beta, email support@oracoach.app.

What to report

Beta feedback is most useful when it includes what happened, what you expected, and what workout or screen you were using.

  • Crashes.
  • Login issues.
  • Workout or session bugs.
  • Set logging issues.
  • Confusing UI.
  • Incorrect exercise or session behavior.
  • Coaching feedback quality issues.
  • Feature requests.
