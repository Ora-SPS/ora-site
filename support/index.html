Beta support

Ora support.

For beta access, product issues, privacy requests, and account deletion requests, email support@oracoach.app.

What to include

Helpful reports include the screen or workout you were using, what happened, what you expected, and whether the issue repeats.

Beta testers should report

  • Crashes.
  • Login issues.
  • Workout or session bugs.
  • Set logging issues.
  • Confusing UI.
  • Incorrect exercise or session behavior.
  • Coaching feedback quality issues.
  • Feature requests.
