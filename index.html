What Ora does

Workout tracking with coaching context.

Log the work

Record exercises, sets, reps, weight, RPE, and rest times without slowing down your session.

Follow progress

See how training volume, performance, and consistency are moving over time.

Know the next step

Get practical guidance for what to do next in the workout and what may need attention in your training week.

Who it is for

Built for lifters who care about the details.

Ora is designed for people who train consistently and want their workout log to become useful feedback, not just a spreadsheet.

It is especially suited for strength training, hypertrophy blocks, progressive overload, and lifters who already track or want to start tracking RPE, load, and session quality.
