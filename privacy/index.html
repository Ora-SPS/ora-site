Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

This is a starter beta privacy policy for Ora. It should be reviewed and updated before a public App Store launch. Ora is an AI workout coach app for serious lifters that helps users track workouts, log training details, monitor progress, and receive coaching guidance.

Information Ora may collect

During beta use, Ora may collect the following information:

  • Account information.
  • Workout logs.
  • Exercise names.
  • Sets, reps, weight, RPE, and rest times.
  • Progress data.
  • App usage data.
  • Crash and debug data.
  • User-submitted feedback.
  • AI-related inputs and outputs if coaching features are used.

How Ora may use information

Ora may use collected information to:

  • Provide workout tracking.
  • Generate coaching guidance.
  • Improve the app.
  • Debug crashes and technical issues.
  • Provide support.

AI coaching features

If you use coaching features, your submitted training context and the related AI-generated outputs may be processed to provide coaching guidance, improve product quality, debug issues, and support your use of the app. Ora does not claim to diagnose, treat, or prevent medical conditions, and coaching guidance is not a substitute for professional medical advice.

Privacy and account deletion requests

You can contact Ora for privacy questions or account deletion requests at support@oracoach.app. Please include enough information for the support team to identify the account or request.

Changes to this policy

Ora may update this policy as the beta changes, new features are added, or legal and operational requirements are reviewed. The updated policy will be posted on this page with a revised date.
