Ora / AI fitness coach
Bounded feedback

Coaching recommendations tied to the evidence.

Ora is designed around structured data, uncertainty, and visible boundaries instead of black-box fitness advice.

What Ora supports

  • Conclusion, confidence, next action, and the evidence used.
  • Missing training, nutrition, progress, or recovery data shown before the recommendation.
  • Safety boundaries for pain, health, and sparse logging context.
  • Clear separation between coach feedback and athlete-owned action.

Why it matters

When data is incomplete, the product should say so. Uncertainty keeps recommendations useful and honest.

AI fitness coach FAQ

How does this connect to reports?

Ora keeps captured data, source quality, and missing context together so review packets can show both signal and uncertainty.

Does Ora replace coaching judgment?

No. Ora organizes evidence and feedback boundaries. Athletes and coaches still decide what to do with the review.

Ora is developed by Raul Valle for lifters who want training, nutrition, progress reports, and coach review in one accountable system.
