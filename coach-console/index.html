Ora / Coach console
Coach review

Coach review without taking control away.

Ora is moving toward coach infrastructure where athletes share report snapshots and coaches respond with bounded feedback.

What Ora supports

  • Athlete controls access and chooses which report snapshot to share.
  • Coach sees scoped summaries, caveats, and review questions.
  • Feedback is tied to evidence instead of silently modifying logs or programs.
  • Athlete reviews recommendations before applying, ignoring, or asking follow-up.

Why it matters

Coach tooling should improve review quality without turning private athlete logs into unrestricted coach workspaces.

Coach console FAQ

How does this connect to reports?

Ora keeps captured data, source quality, and missing context together so review packets can show both signal and uncertainty.

Does Ora replace coaching judgment?

No. Ora organizes evidence and feedback boundaries. Athletes and coaches still decide what to do with the review.

Ora is developed by Raul Valle for lifters who want training, nutrition, progress reports, and coach review in one accountable system.
