Support Ora.

Ora is a local-first, privacy-respecting fitness app with a research spine. It is built by a small team and kept independent on purpose. If Ora is useful to you, you can help keep it that way — with a one-time or recurring donation, or an organization sponsorship.

Where your support goes

Support covers the real, modest costs of running an independent app and keeps the core experience free for everyone.

  • Infrastructure: hosting, database, secure storage, and email.
  • Keeping core training, diet, and progress logging free.
  • The research behind Ora — trend analysis for strength, hypertrophy, and endurance.
  • Runway so the team can keep building without selling you out.

What support does not do

This is a trust boundary, not a tagline:

  • It never unlocks features that should be free. Donations are not a paywall.
  • It never puts ads inside the app.
  • It never means selling or sharing your data. Privacy, export, and delete are always free.

Two ways to help

Individuals. A one-time or recurring donation of any size helps. You can opt in to a public thank-you on our backers list, or stay anonymous.

Organizations. Sponsorships from aligned companies — privacy, fitness, strength science, health tech, cloud and AI — get tasteful acknowledgement on the site and in release notes. No in-app placement, no user data. Acknowledgement is editorial, on our terms.
