Report console

Reports that keep caveats attached.

Ora reports combine training, nutrition, progress, and data-quality context into exportable review packets for athletes and trusted coaches.

What Ora supports

  • Training load, completed sets, duration, adherence, and observed log signals.
  • Nutrition coverage, protein target consistency, estimate-heavy days, and sparse-log warnings.
  • Progress timeline with body metrics, upload recency, and check-in cadence.
  • JSON snapshots, CSV tables, markdown coach packets, and browser print workflows.

Why it matters

Reports should make decisions easier while showing what data the athlete and coach should not overtrust.

Export and report FAQ

How does this connect to reports?

Ora keeps captured data, source quality, and missing context together so review packets can show both signal and uncertainty.

Does Ora replace coaching judgment?

No. Ora organizes evidence and feedback boundaries. Athletes and coaches still decide what to do with the review.

Ora is built for lifters who want training, nutrition, progress reports, and coach review in one accountable system.
