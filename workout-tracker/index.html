Workout tracker

The workout tracker that understands your training.

Log sets, reps, load, RPE and RIR in seconds — then let Ora trend your strength and volume, flag fatigue, and adjust today’s session before you waste it. Built for serious lifters, not streaks and confetti.

What you can track

  • Sets, reps, load, RPE and RIR — fast enough to keep up with the session.
  • Estimated 1RM trends on the squat, bench and deadlift.
  • Hard sets per muscle and weekly volume for hypertrophy.
  • Plan-vs-actual: completed, modified, skipped and substituted work.
  • Pain, fatigue and missing context — flagged before any recommendation.

More than a log

A plain log records what you did. Ora turns it into your next step: it watches your strength and volume trends, catches fatigue before it compounds, and can draft a change to today’s work — with the reason and how sure it is — for you to accept or ignore. Workout tracker FAQ

Is it good for powerlifting and bodybuilding?

Yes — Ora is built for powerbuilding. It tracks RPE/RIR and estimated 1RM for strength, and hard sets per muscle and weekly volume for hypertrophy.

Can it adjust my session?

Ora can draft a change to today’s work with one reason and a confidence level. Nothing changes until you apply it — you stay in control.

Does it trend e1RM and volume for me?

Yes, from the sets you log. Ora shows the numbers with their caveats and labels estimates rather than pretending one session proves a trend.

How much does it cost?

There’s a Free tier to start and Ora Plus at a proposed $4.99/mo. Billing is not live during the private beta.
